Pikachu

Whenever Pikachu comes across something new, it blasts it with a jolt of electricity. If you come across a blackened berry, it's evidence that this Pokémon mistook the intensity of its charge. Evolved from Pichu (nº 172), and its final stage is Raichu (nº026) reached when you use a Thunderstone.

You can find more information about this Pokémon at Bulbapedia, details that directly talks about the game and anime. While at Serebii you can find technical details of this creature, such as moves learned by HM or level and so on.
